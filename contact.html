Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How can I place an order for school uniforms?

You can place an order for school uniforms by contacting us through our website, email, or phone. We'll guide you through the process, including measurements, customization options, and delivery details. For bulk orders, we offer special pricing and dedicated account management.

What digital marketing services do you offer for real estate?

Our digital marketing services for real estate include AI-powered property listing optimization, virtual tour creation, targeted social media campaigns, SEO for real estate websites, lead generation systems, email marketing, and comprehensive analytics. We tailor our strategies to meet the specific needs of real estate professionals.

How long does a typical life coaching program last?

The duration of our life coaching programs varies based on individual needs and goals. Typically, clients work with us for 3-6 months with sessions every 1-2 weeks. Some clients prefer ongoing support and continue with monthly sessions after completing their initial program. We offer a complimentary discovery call to determine the best approach for your specific situation.

Do you offer consultations before starting a project?

Yes, we offer complimentary consultations for all our services. During this initial meeting, we'll discuss your needs, goals, and how our services can help you achieve them. This allows us to create a customized proposal and ensures we're the right fit for your project before any commitment is made.

What areas do you serve?

For our school uniform business, we primarily serve local and regional schools, with shipping available nationwide. Our digital marketing services and life coaching are available globally, with virtual meetings and consultations available for clients anywhere in the world. In-person coaching sessions are available for clients in our local area.
