Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our uniform business

How do we place bulk orders for our school?

Bulk orders can be placed through our dedicated school portal or by contacting our sales team directly. We offer special pricing and customization options for bulk school orders. Our team will guide you through the entire process from measurement to delivery.

What brands do you offer?

We partner with multiple trusted brands in the school uniform industry to provide a wide range of options. Some of our partner brands include SchoolWear, EduCloth, SmartStep Shoes, and ClassicUniform. All our products meet quality and durability standards required for school wear.

Do you offer customization for school logos?

Yes, we offer comprehensive customization services including embroidery and printing of school logos, names, and other identifiers on uniforms. We work closely with schools to ensure that all branding requirements are met with precision and quality.

What is your delivery timeframe?

Standard delivery for individual orders typically takes 3-5 business days. For bulk school orders, delivery timeframes are determined based on order volume and customization requirements, usually ranging from 2-4 weeks. We also offer express delivery options for urgent requirements at an additional cost.

How do you handle returns and exchanges?

We have a customer-friendly return and exchange policy. Items in original condition can be returned within 30 days of purchase. Size exchanges are processed promptly to ensure students have properly fitting uniforms. For bulk orders, we work with schools to establish specific return protocols that meet their needs.
